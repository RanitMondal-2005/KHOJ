How Khoj Works

A straightforward 3-step process connecting families, hospitals, and police.

Step 1
Family Files a Report

A family member registers and submits a missing person report — physical description, photos, district, and contact details.

Step 2
Hospital Uploads a Patient

Hospital staff adds a record for an unidentified patient — estimated age, physical features, found location, and photos.

Step 3
System Finds Matches

Khoj's matching engine scores compatibility across gender, age, blood group, district, marks, and more. Families and hospitals are notified of potential matches instantly.

Who Uses Khoj

Three distinct roles, each with their own secure dashboard.

Families

File up to 3 active missing person reports. Add search updates and clues. Get notified when a potential hospital match is found.

Hospital Staff

Upload unidentified patient records with photos. Receive alerts when a family match is found. Mark patients as identified.

Police Officers

Browse regional missing cases and nearby hospital matches. Use as an investigation support and coordination tool.

Important: Khoj is a coordination and support tool only. It does not replace official police investigation systems, FIR registration, or any legal process. Always file a proper FIR with your local police station when reporting a missing person.
